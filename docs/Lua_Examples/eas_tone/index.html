--[[
Title: EAS Tones
Author: TheSinkGod
Updated: February 1 2020
Description: This example script generates two overlapping frequencies that generate the attention tone you hear at the beginning of a US emergency alert, for 2 seconds.
Difficulty: 1
Intended Version: Alpha 1 (Build 100)
--]]

PlayRawFrequency("sine", 853, 2000)
PlayRawFrequency("sine", 960, 2000)

Breakdown

  1. Two functions are ran that play their respective sine waves for 2000 ms (2 seconds).
